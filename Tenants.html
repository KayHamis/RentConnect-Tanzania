Tenant Responsibilities

Understand your responsibilities such as paying rent on time, maintaining the property, and respecting neighbors.

Your Rights Include:

  • Right to safe and habitable housing
  • Privacy and peaceful enjoyment
  • Protection from discriminatory practices
  • Proper notice before eviction
  • Return of security deposit
  • Essential services maintenance

Your Responsibilities:

  • Pay rent on time each month
  • Keep the property clean and maintained
  • Report maintenance issues promptly
  • Respect neighbors and community rules
  • Follow lease agreement terms
  • Give proper notice before moving out

Important Documents:

  • Signed lease agreement
  • Security deposit receipt
  • Rent payment records
  • Property inspection reports
  • Communication with landlord
  • Insurance documentation
